Vs. Recorder
A Vs. Recorder is a device that allows you to record battles[0x000A]such as Link Battles and battles at the Battle Spot.[0x000A]It also allows you to replay battles or reenact them in mock[0x000A]battles against the same opponents again.[0x000A]If you use the PC in the castle in Festival Plaza, you can[0x000A]make your Battle Videos public or view Battle Videos that[0x000A]are made public via the Internet.
You can make your Battle Videos recorded by your Vs.[0x000A]Recorder public, or search and view Battle Videos that[0x000A]are made public by other players.[0x000A]A Battle Video that is made public has a search code.[0x000A]Share the code with other people, and they can view the[0x000A]same video even if they are far away.
